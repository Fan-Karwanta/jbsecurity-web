About Us

Our Company Story

In the aftermath of the dissolution of the Inter-con Security Agency on August 15, 2018, the company's shareholders convened to establish a new company. JB Pentagon Security and Investigation Agency, Inc. was registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) on August 30, 2018. We have been providing proactive, quality, and reliable services since 2016.
